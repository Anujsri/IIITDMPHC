IIITDMJ PHC Guidelines

New Guidelines 12-12-2017

Emergency Services

Ambulance services can be used in case of medical emergency or under special circumstances where the movement of a person is difficult or unbearable pain without an ambulance. Hostel residents need to contact the Caretaker of respective Halls caretaker shall make a call to the PHC for the ambulance. Campus residents can call to PHC for ambulance service.

PHC : 0761- 279 4187

Ambulance Schedule

It is responsibility of all to ensure that the ambulance is not kept waiting for more than 10-15 minutes so that other needy persons are not deprived of the service. Please remember that ambulance services are to help the campus residents in emergency situation or in case of extreme difficulty. Any misuse of these services may lead to a fine of Rs. 200/500 (inside/outside the campus respectively). The final decision in this regard shall be taken by the Faculty In-charge, PHC.

Ambulance No. : 9425805538

Online Appointment

For emergency medical assistance, the campus residents may be referred to any hospitals in city only after a health check-up at the Primary Health Center in our campus. Decision to send ambulance outside the campus will be taken by Doctor / Nursing Assistants on duty at PHC. For non-emergency cases, if required, campus residents may send their vehicle request (on payment basis) to AR (GA).
